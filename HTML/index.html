Through fast, easy, and hassle-free online loans and financial services, we live with our promise of making every Filipinos’ dreams possible through lending. Blend PH offers the best online loans that will surely fit every borrowers’ standards. Our easy cash loans are comprised of Fast Loan, Salary Loan, Personal Loan, Seafarer Loan, and Franchise Loans. These loans are available for online application and their length of repayment and interests varies from 1-36 months with 1-8% interest per month depending on the loan product that you are going to avail. We made also made our terms flexible to cater to a broader scope of borrowers and lenders. We believe that our customers should always feel at ease about their mode of repayment to establish our hassle-free and convenient cash loans.
Since we are an online funding platform, we cater to two different audiences, the borrower and the lenders. Lenders can invest their money through the online lending option of the platform while, the borrowers can ask for quick and easy cash loans to these online lenders. Blend PH bridges the gap between the borrowers and the lenders anywhere in the Philippines by serving as a channel that processes and matches these two audiences based on each of the other’s requirements.

What are online loans?
You can get your online loans in our peer-to-peer funding platform. Blend PH offers a variety of online loan products that you can avail of. Just follow these quick and easy steps for you to avail your desired online loan.
1. Check your eligibility by logging in to app.blend.ph
2. Register as a borrower and accomplish all the needed information for evaluation
3. Complete the loan application by uploading all the necessary documents and answering all the credit questions
4. Upon submission, underwriting team will evaluate your application and requirements for endorsement to our partner lenders for funding
5. Upon approval, your cash will be disbursed immediately. The processing time for evaluation can vary from 24-48 hours depending on the completeness of your submitted requirements and your accomplished questions from our website.

For lenders, you can also apply online on our website. These few simple steps will also help you accomplish your online application.

1. Go to app.blend.ph/register.
2. Register as an investor.
3. Accomplish all the necessary information from documents to questions.
4. Once verified, you will receive an email that will provide instructions on how you can start your Blend wallet.
5. Upon crediting your monetary load to your Blend wallet, you can now start funding online loan applications that suit your specifications.
