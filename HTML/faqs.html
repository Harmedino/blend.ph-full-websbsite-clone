How to avoid scammers?

• Spot the red flags. Scammers may use a corporate brand or organization’s name and pretend to represent one. If they dangle gifts, offer reduced fees, or pressure you to make an upfront investment, refuse.
• Do not take the bait. Some scammers use bait, like fake tickets, to get your money. Or prey on unsuspecting victims’ emotions, then ask for fund transfer. Others state many people have accepted their offer. Avoid giving personal information/bank details.
• Do not be duped by the sense of urgency they create. Scammers may use tactics, like timing their move during or after a tragedy or disaster. If the offer is too good to be true, trust your instinct and steer clear!
• Verify identities. Always recheck a person’s name or company being dealt with. Is the information being provided verifiable? Confirm claims by directly calling the company mentioned.
