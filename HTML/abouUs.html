Blend.PH
BlendPH is developed and managed by Inclusive Financial Technologies, as an online platform to connect borrowers who need access to a financial institution to fund their goals and lenders who want to grow their wealth through intelligible investing.
OUR SOCIAL

RESPONSIBILITY

As one of the pioneers of peer-to-peer, our social responsibility thrust is to promote responsible Borrowing and ethical Lending in the Philippines. Thus, Blend.PH not only promotes the use of flexible, low-interest loans, our company also advertises empathy and camaraderie through investing and financially assisting each other. As a peer-to-peer institution, we act as an avenue where our investors grow their money and at the same time, help lift the lives of their fellow-men.

Jay S. Bautista
Managing Director

With his 25-year experience in business management and finance under his belt, Jay S. Bautista founded BlendPH, a fintech start-up and the first online peer-to-peer lending platform in the Philippines.

Bautista and the rest of the BlendPH team aim to provide financial inclusivity for lenders seeking an investment opportunity and borrowers looking for alternative financial facilities in the Philippines.

While Bautista keeps the momentum of his success in BlendPH, he also shares his expertise with other organizations. This endeavor includes being the Founder and Managing Director of Insys Technologies Inc., the President of Transport Technology Holdings Corp., and the Director of Diversified Capital Inc.

Bautista's triumphs in finance and business management are backed by years of experience in other financial institutions he built and led, like Conversia Inc. and Alternative Payment Portal Philippines Inc.

He finished his Agricultural Business and Management degree at the University of the Philippines in Los Ba√±os.

Ronnie T. Ong
Member

Ronnie T. Ong is one of the board members of BlendPH. His extensive experience in business management and consultancy has always contributed to the success of BlendPH.

Ong is currently one of the consultants of Merrymart and an emerging consumer- focused retail company principally engaged in the operation of retail stores in the supermarket and household essentials category. Before this, he also held the Chief Business Development Adviser position at Robinson Supermarket Corporation.

Ong also served as the Director of Diversified Capital Inc. Before this, he served as the Executive Vice President of Conversion and the Managing Director of Alternative Payment Portal Philippines Inc.

Ong is a Bachelor of Science in Commerce Major in Accounting at the University of San Jose-Recoletos Cebu.

Marcelino Michael I.
Atanante IV
Member

Atty. Marcelino Michael I. Atanante IV is another board member of BlendPH. His law practice over the years is focused on Criminal and Civil litigation, Tax Assessments and Tax Litigation, Contracts and Corporate Law, Election Law, Immigration Law, Aviation Law, and International Commercial Transactions. His knowledge of the law has been proven to steer BlendPH to success.

Currently, he serves as the Government Affairs Manager of Cebu Pacific Air and a Partner at Gargantiel, Ilagan & Atalante. But before this, he served as the legal counsel to a few politicians.

Atty. Atalante IV finished his law at the University of Sto. Tomas and his undergraduate at UP Diliman.

Manuel H. Torres
Member

Manuel H. Torres, the former Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Philippine National Railways (PNR), is a current board member of BlendPH. His long list of success in business administration has been helping BlendPH pursue its endeavor of providing financial inclusivity to Filipinos.

Among the many organizations where Torres made a name include Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP)_ where he served as the Assistant Governor.

Before his stay at BSP, Torres also held Vice Presidential positions in State Properties Corp., Leighton Contractors Philippines, Inc., and San Miguel properties Philippines, Inc.

Torres finished his Master's degree in Business Administration at the University of the Philippines. He also completed his undergraduate in Mechanical Engineering at UP.
